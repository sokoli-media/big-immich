# Big Immich

**Community tvOS app for Immich (Apple TV)**

> A simple, free community app to display Immich photo and video slideshows on your Apple TV.

This is a **community-driven project** and is **not affiliated with Immich**.

## ğŸ¯ Why Big Immich exists

Showing photos and videos from Immich on a TV used to be surprisingly painful:
* AirPlay from a browser was unreliable
* HDMI cables were inconvenient
* Immich had (or maybe still has) some bugs with showing slideshow
* Existing alternatives were not working at all or required paid subscriptions just to show slideshows (why?!)

Big Immich was created to solve exactly one problem: **a simple, reliable way to enjoy Immich albums on an Apple TV**.
